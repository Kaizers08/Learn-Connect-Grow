┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  🔥 KEY ACTIONS                                                  │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  MENTOR:
  ───────
  1. Click "➕ Upload Material" → Upload modal opens
  2. Fill form + select file → Upload
  3. Click mentee card → See their progress
  4. Edit/Delete materials from modal
  5. Track all mentees' progress


  MENTEE:
  ───────
  1. Click mentor card → Materials modal opens
  2. See all materials with checkboxes
  3. Click checkbox → Mark as complete
  4. Progress bar updates automatically
  5. Repeat for all materials → 100% 🎉
